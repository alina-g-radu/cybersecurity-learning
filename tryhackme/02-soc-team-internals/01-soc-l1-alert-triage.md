# SOC L1 Alert Triage

## What I learned
- Security events are continuously generated, but only specific events are escalated into alerts.
- Alert triage involves filtering and prioritizing alerts based on severity, time, and potential impact.

## Key concepts
- SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) is used to collect, analyze, and correlate security events.
- EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response) focuses on detecting and responding to threats on endpoint devices.

## Why it matters in SOC
- Alert triage ensures that the most critical threats are identified and handled first, helping prevent serious security incidents.

## Tools / activities used
- Practiced triaging alerts using a simplified SIEM environment.

## Personal notes
- Alert triage requires careful analysis to avoid missing real threats while also reducing false positives.
