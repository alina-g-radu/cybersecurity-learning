# SOC Workbooks and Lookups

## What I learned
- SOC analysts use supporting resources such as identity inventories, asset inventories, and network diagrams to better understand suspicious activity.
- These resources help determine whether an alert is a false positive or a true positive.

## Key concepts
- Identity inventory: A catalogue of employees, user accounts, and services within an organization.
- Asset inventory (asset lookup): A record of hardware, software, and systems used in the organization.
- Network diagram: A visual representation of the network structure and connections between systems.
- SOC workbook / playbook / runbook / workflow: Step-by-step instructions used to investigate and respond to specific types of threats.

## Why it matters in SOC
- Having quick access to accurate information allows analysts to triage alerts faster and make better decisions.

## Tools / activities used
- Practiced analyzing scenarios using SOC workbooks and supporting data.

## Personal notes
- Structured resources like playbooks and inventories make investigations more efficient and reduce uncertainty during alert analysis.
