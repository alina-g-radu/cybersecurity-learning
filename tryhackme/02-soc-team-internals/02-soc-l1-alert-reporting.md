# SOC L1 Alert Reporting

## What I learned
- Alert reporting is important, especially when an alert requires escalation.
- An alert report should follow the Five Ws framework to ensure clarity and completeness.

## Key concepts
- Five Ws: Who, What, When, Where, Why — a structured approach for documenting incidents clearly.

## Why it matters in SOC
- Detailed reporting ensures that the reasoning behind closing or escalating an alert is clearly documented and can be reviewed later.

## Tools / activities used
- Practiced writing structured alert reports based on different security scenarios.

## Personal notes
- Soft skills such as clear communication and structured writing are essential in SOC work, alongside technical skills.
