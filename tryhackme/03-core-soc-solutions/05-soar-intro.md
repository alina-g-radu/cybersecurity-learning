# Introduction to SOAR

## What I learned

- SOC teams face challenges such as alert fatigue, disconnected tools, manual processes, and limited resources.
- SOAR helps address these challenges by automating workflows and integrating different security tools.

## Key concepts

- Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response (SOAR): A platform that connects security tools, automates tasks, and streamlines incident response.
- Playbooks: Predefined sets of actions used to investigate and respond to specific types of incidents.

## Why it matters in SOC

- SOAR reduces manual workload, improves response time, and helps analysts handle a high volume of alerts more efficiently.

## Tools / activities used

- Explored SOAR concepts and how playbooks are used to automate incident response.

## Personal notes

- SOAR can significantly reduce repetitive tasks, allowing SOC analysts to focus more on analysis and decision-making.
